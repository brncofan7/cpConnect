People
We value people of all ages as the reason for the churches existence.

Teamwork
We value a culture that embraces teamwork. We are passionate about creating a place for everyone to be involved and use their talents for one common goal.

Excellence
We value excellence in who we are and all that we do.

Diversity
We value diversity in age, gender, culture, and race.

Relevance
We value presenting an unchanging message to a changing culture. We desire to relate to our society and present the gospel in a relevant, attractive , contagious environment.

Loyalty
We value loyalty to God, a Christ-centered lifestyle, to others, to the house, and to commitments.

Positive Culture
We value a church that has a positive, uplifting, and welcoming atmosphere. We embrace positive attitudes, faith-filled optimism, and an attractive, contagious environment.

Balance and Growth
We have helping individuals promote balance, growth, and health in Christ-centered lifestyles:

  • Financially
    Several passages in scripture encourage us to be good stewards of our earthly wealth. Center Point Church strongly encourages thoughtful financial stewardship (management) in life.
  • Relationally
    Center Point Church strongly emphasizes the importance of deeply committed family life and strong, healthy relationships with others.
  • Educationally
    As a part of a balanced life, we value and encourage education, and continued personal growth.
  • Physically
    Center Point Church believes that the physical aspect of our lives is important. We emphasize health consciousness, and discourage practices that destroy our bodies.
  • Spiritually
    Jesus grew in favor with God. Jesus knew the importance of keeping his spiritual life on track through prayer, fasting, scripture reading, and spending time in the synagogue. This, of course, is our central concern at Center Point Church.
